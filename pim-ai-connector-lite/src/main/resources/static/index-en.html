🧠 How It Works

The PIM-AI Connector transforms structured product data, existing descriptions, and product images into high-quality, SEO-optimized content – ideal for webshops, catalogs, and PIM systems.

  • 🔧 For Developers: Microservice architecture (Spring Boot), REST API, JSON input, OpenAI & Google Vision integration.
  • 📈 For Project Managers: Ready to use, no copywriters needed, multilingual capabilities.
  • 🛍️ For E-Commerce: Unified, sales-optimized content in seconds.
